Suicide Squad (1987-1992)

Suicide Squad (2001-2002)

Spinning out of "Our Worlds at War" comes the return of the DC Universe's deadliest team! When former members of the Injustice Gang are given a chance for freedom, they're jumping at the chance to sign up for the newest incarnation of the Suicide Squad!



Suicide Squad (2007-2008)

Original Suicide Squad member Rick Flag Jr. was the victim of a nuclear blast while battling terrorism, but now he's back and people are demanding answers. And with the Squad returning to active duty, some secrets will finally be revealed!



Suicide Squad (2011-2014)

They're a team of death-row super villains recruited by the government to take on missions so dangerous that they're sheer suicide. Harley Quinn! Deadshot! King Shark! The villains of the DC Universe star in their own deadly and dangerous series!



This series is rated Teen+.



Suicide Squad (2016-)

Soldier. War hero. Traitor. Captain Rick Flag was one of America's greatest military commanders before he was banished to a secret military prison. But after years of isolation, Flag's life changes forever when a woman called Amanda Waller offers him redemption in exchange for taking on the single most dangerous job in the entire DC Universe: keeping the Suicide Squad alive!
