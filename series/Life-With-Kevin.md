LIFE WITH KEVIN introduces readers to an older, wiser and worldly Kevin Keller as he explores the big city with his BFF Veronica. A coming-of-age story that is both heartfelt and funny, LIFE WITH KEVIN reintroduces one of Archie's most popular characters in a new setting with a whole new cast of characters -- plus a handful of familiar faces! Written and drawn by Kevin's creator, Dan Parent, with inks from the legendary J. Bone.
