Red Tornado (1985)

Spurned by the JLA, ostracized by all humanity, the Red Tornado must face the Construct alone! This superhero, an android, a robot body possessed of the elemental force of a tornado, feels alienated from the human race but protective of it. He's the only one who can tackle the Construct - that disembodied electronic consciousness cohered out of the network of electronic signals that fill Earth's atmosphere - intent on enslaving the will of every living creature be it animal, human or superhero. Can the Red Tornado, a machine himself, save the world from the Construct? And can he finally discover his value to humanity?
